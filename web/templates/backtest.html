RSI
🤖 BTCUSDT PRO_1
RSI Swing <30/>70 · Scale-In · Profit-Locked Exit
BTC/USDT 1m+ Scale-In ▼
1
📈 ENTRY — RSI closes below 30
  • Candle closes with RSI < 30 and no open position → BUY $5 of BTC
  • Only free USDT cash is used. No margin, no leverage, ever.
2
➕ SCALE-IN — RSI dips below 30 again (while still in trade)
  • RSI drops below 30 again before a sell has been triggered
  • Bot checks real free USDT balance in real-time:
  • Free balance ≥ $5 → BUY another $5 — avg entry price recalculated (weighted average of all fills)
  • Free balance < $5 → HOLD — skip the trade, never borrow
3
📉 SELL — RSI closes above 70 AND trade is in green
  • BOTH must be true on the same candle close:
  • RSI > 70
  • Current price ≥ average entry price (even $0.001 profit counts — break-even also closes)
  • All accumulated BTC is sold in a single order
4
🔒 PROFIT-LOCK — RSI > 70 but still in loss
  • Bot engages PROFIT-LOCK mode — does NOT sell
  • Every candle it checks: RSI still > 70 AND price now ≥ avg entry → SELL
  • If RSI drops back below 70 while still in loss → just hold (no sell, no new buys)
  • Once RSI crosses back above 70 and trade is finally green → SELL
  • No new scale-ins while PROFIT-LOCK is active
5
🔄 CYCLE RESET
  • After any successful sell → back to IDLE, wait for next RSI < 30
⚙️ Protections
✅ Never sells at a loss — stop-loss at –2% still protects against hard crashes
✅ Never uses margin or leverage — balance check is enforced in real-time on every cycle
✅ Scale-in avg cost is always correctly recalculated as a weighted average of all fills
📋 Example Scenario
Candle 1: RSI=28 → BUY $5 @ $85,000
Candle 4: RSI=26 again → balance=$5 → BUY $5 more @ $84,200 → avg entry=$84,600
Candle 9: RSI=72 but price=$84,100 (loss) → PROFIT-LOCK engaged — hold
Candle 12: RSI=65 → below 70, still holding — no action
Candle 15: RSI=71 but price=$84,500 (still loss) → still locked — hold
Candle 18: RSI=73 AND price=$84,650 (green!) → SELL ALL — cycle complete ✓

MACD
📊 MACD Histogram (3/15/3)
Momentum histogram crossover — good on 4H
4H recommended ▼
BUY
MACD Histogram crosses from negative to positive
  • Fast EMA (3) crosses above Slow EMA (15) after being below
  • Signal line (3-period EMA of MACD) confirmation
  • Histogram bar flips positive — entry on close of that candle
SELL
MACD Histogram crosses from positive to negative
  • Fast EMA crosses back below Slow EMA
  • Histogram bar flips negative — exit on close of that candle
  • No profit-lock: exits immediately on signal regardless of P&L
⚙️ Notes
📌Best on 4H or 1D — too noisy on 1m
📌Parameters: Fast=3, Slow=15, Signal=3
📌Trend-following — performs best in trending markets, struggles in chop
Adjust EMA periods and save as a custom Quick Launch preset
TEMA
⭐ TEMA Crossover (9/21) + ADX
Research pick · Sharpe ~13 in backtests · ADX trend filter
★ Sharpe 13 1H–4H ▼
BUY
Fast TEMA (9) crosses above Slow TEMA (21) with ADX > 20
  • Triple EMA (9) crosses above Triple EMA (21)
  • ADX > 20 confirms a trending market — no trades in sideways chop
  • Entry on candle close
SELL
Fast TEMA (9) crosses below Slow TEMA (21)
  • Triple EMA (9) drops back below Triple EMA (21)
  • ADX check not required on exit
⚙️ Notes
📌TEMA reacts faster than EMA/SMA — fewer whipsaws in trending markets
📌ADX filter is the key edge — removes most false signals in range conditions
📌Backtested Sharpe of ~13 is exceptional — validate with fresh data before live
Tune TEMA periods and ADX filter → save as your own Quick Launch preset
ARB
⚡ Arbitrage Ratio (BTC/ETH)
Statistical mean-reversion on the BTC÷ETH price ratio
BTC + ETH 4H+ ▼
BUY
Ratio drops below mean − 2σ (BTC cheap vs ETH)
  • Computes BTC/ETH price ratio each candle
  • Rolling SMA(30) and standard deviation of the ratio
  • When ratio < SMA − 2σ → BUY BTC (statistically undervalued vs ETH)
SELL
Ratio reverts above mean + 2σ (BTC expensive vs ETH)
  • When ratio > SMA + 2σ → SELL BTC
  • Mean-reversion trade completes when ratio returns to historical norm
⚙️ Notes
📌Requires both BTC and ETH price feeds simultaneously
📌Works best when BTC and ETH are highly correlated — breaks down in divergence events
📌Live ratio monitor available on the Trading page
Adjust the SMA window and Z-score thresholds → save as your own preset
